about us

Established in 2017, Bueno Law has rapidly grown into a leading advocate for clients across diverse legal landscapes. With a dedicated team of over 50 professionals, we have proudly served more than 1,000 clients, offering expert guidance and personalized solutions. Our commitment to excellence and client satisfaction drives us to continuously deliver top-tier legal services, ensuring that every case receives the attention and expertise it deserves.

Who are the Bueno Law Warriors?

At Bueno Law, our team embodies a profound commitment to justice and empathy, shaped by our personal journeys as immigrants and refugees. Founded by Fernanda Bueno, who herself endured the harrowing experience of seeking safety in the U.S., our firm is a sanctuary for those navigating the complexities of immigration. We are a diverse group hailing from Brazil, El Salvador, Mexico, Guatemala, and Argentina, and we bring a wealth of shared experiences and cultural insights to our practice. Each member of our team has faced their own struggles and triumphs, allowing us to deeply understand and relate to the challenges our clients face. Whether working locally in California or remotely, we unite with unwavering determination to fight for the safety and freedom of our clients, always fiercely advocating for their best interests. At Bueno Law, we are not just legal professionals; we are dedicated warriors standing by your side, ensuring that fear has no place in your journey towards a brighter future.
